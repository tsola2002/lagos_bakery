Our Philosophy

The purposes of the business are to provide sustainable employment for co-op members and staff.

to contribute to our local economy through purchasing locally and providing nutritious food, to function as an active, supportive member of our community, and to be as earth friendly as possible, supporting fair trade and sustainable agriculture.

Our Mission

At Lagos Bakery all the flour, seeds, vegetables, most fruits, eggs, and many other ingredients used in our bakeries are certified organic and as local as we can source. The remaining ingredients are as pure and natural as possible.

Buying Organic to us means supporting sustainable agriculture without the use of herbicides, pesticides, and chemical fertilizers which can damage soil micro-organisms and pollute our drinking water

What We Do

At Lagos Bakery we sppecialized in the production of bakery items and also take orders for the bakery items as well serve bakery items in our cafe which opens in the morning.

We also do custom catering for events & functions such as weddings, banquets birthday parties, bridal showers. our aim is to give our clients a full premium experience.

Our Team

Our highly skilled bakers and award winning cake designers are professionally trained and have many years of experience in baking and cake design. Vanilla Bake Shop cakes, cupcakes and confections are baked from scratch with only the highest quality butter, cream, sugar, flour, eggs, Nielson Massey Vanilla Beans, Guittard and Callebaut chocolates.
